About ASIA KINGHT Cosplay

Want to build costumes? We’ll show you how!

Hey there! I am Trinh Daniel !

My costumes adventure started in 2016 and my passion yet!

I spend most of my time bmaking costumes and props from my favourite games, anime or movies. I love sharing my knowledge and crafting experience with the rest of the community through my books, videos, blog posts and tutorials.


Crafting is my life and I’re very thankful i can do this as my job.

WHAT IS COSPLAY?

Cosplay is Love!

For me, cosplay is the best way I can express my love for a fictional world or character.

Instead of just playing a game or watching a movie, I try to bring the character to life! And I’re not alone. Cosplay connects an huge worldwide community of passionate artists.


I don’t know gender, age, religion, skin color or body shape. It’s not about what separates us, but what we all have in common:

Dressing up and having fun!
